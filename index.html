Why use this ?


Think about one day you find an interesting book in a bookstore,
and want to check whether the same copy is in the nearby libraries or not.
But wait, it's so bothering to visit those libraries' webpage one by one! isn't it?
This is why we build this app, which collects ALL those infomation for you, in just ONE page! with just ONE click!

Librarian, your mobile library !

See what you can do with this app !

First, open this app on your phone.

Then click the ISBN scaner button and
scan the ISBN barcode on the book.

Get the information about the book and
its presence in nearby libraries.
Simple like this!
